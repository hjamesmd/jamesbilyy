## REPORT
James Bilyy and Ivan Kit were graduates of the Lviv Polythechnic University. They moved in together shortly before graduating, seeming like normal friends to their acquaintances.

On January 9th, 2023, James Bilyy had successfully transferred his mind into his PC. Had Ivan Kit not intervened, he would have been declared brain-dead. Over the next two months, Ivan developed an AI based off James while keeping his corpse on life support using stolen equipment. Using undisclosed techniques, Ivan was able to embed a processor unit into the lifeless body, and it started sending signals again, effectively resuscitating James. 

Even though this method worked, Ivan ended up being dissatisfied with the result, as the AI could not capture who James really was. While he tried to make it act more like the real person, the results were minimal. On May 9th, 2023, Ivan wiped the memory of the AI and sent it to Vienna, Austria with fabricated documents. 

Now named James Weiß, he applied to the Vienna State University and got accepted. He became a successful mathematician and pursued Minecraft content creation as a hobby. Nobody was aware of him being an AI, and he garnered some popularity due to his unique ability to read or write any ASCII-based cipher without a cheat sheet or calculator. 

One day in 2026, James Weiß woke up to his worlds being deleted save for one that was not there before. When he opened it, he found a disc with a recording of his voice that he had never heard before. Upon further exploration, he found a lot of confusing books and texts. The entire time he was being watched by James Bilyy, as he manifested as a simple player in the world. 

The world is a projection of Bilyy's mind as it exists on hardware, which is why it seems warped and complex. Inside of the world, he put Weiß through a few challenges to see if it was really him. Based on his own words, he learnt about what Ivan had done and wanted to find his body. 

After Weiß completed all of Bilyy's challenges, Bilyy decided that he would get rid of Weiß altogether. He challenged him to an in-game duel, to which he agreed, and used his pc to send a strong electrical current. Weiß ended up being electrocuted and shut down. In the next few days a concerned neighbour called the authorities to investigate his apartment, and he was declared dead the same day. His PC was wiped and there remained only a text file saying "He was long overdue." and a video of him being electrocuted.

As of the day of writing this, there is no concensus about what exactly Bilyy was trying to achieve.
